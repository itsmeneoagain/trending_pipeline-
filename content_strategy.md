## Hook Templates

```
KISI NE NOTICE KIYA?:
"Bhai, [game] mein ek cheez hai jo developer ne khud confirm ki — aur sirf 1% log jaante hain."

GAMER IS BEING REPLACED:
"Ye game tumhe apni hi [job/skill] se replace karna sikhata hai."

PHIR BHI DOOB GAYA:
"[X] million wishlists thi / [budget] tha, phir bhi doob gaya."

HIDDEN GEM INTRO:
"Ye game exist karta hai aur koi baat nahi karta. Aaj hum batate hain."

POORA SAFAR:
"[X] saal. [Y] games. [Franchise] ka poora safar."

99% LOGON NE MISS KIYA:
"[Movie] trailer mein [game] ka easter egg chhupa hai jo director ne confirm kiya."

KOI BAAT KYUN NAHI KAR RAHA?:
"[Game] aa raha hai [date] ko — aur koi baat kyun nahi kar raha?"

Rs X NE Rs Y KO HARAYA:
"Rs[X] ke game ne Rs[Y] AAA ko kaise haraya?"

ASLI KAHANI:
"[Budget] ka budget, [timeline] mein khatam. [Game] ki asli kahani."

GAME EXPLAINED:
"[Game] explained — poori [story/journey/mechanic] 60 seconds mein."
```

## Content Tiers

- **T1 — Hidden Gem:** Zero or near-zero Hinglish coverage. Games like Hacknet, Star Trucker, Bear & Breakfast, NO LAW, Mina the Hollower, Cairn. Intro format or KISI NE NOTICE KIYA.
- **T2 — Big-Search Adjacency:** Massive games (GTA, Forza, AC) covered from an oblique angle. Never the obvious take — find the Indian pricing angle, the failure story, the hidden detail.
- **T3 — Themed Lists / Explainers:** Genre lists with Indian context, franchise history, gaming culture explainers.

---

## Off-Lane Content (skip or oblique angle only)

- GTA roleplay / Franklin + Shinchan type content
- BGMI / Free Fire ranked push, crate openings, UC
- Live streams and rank push videos
- Minecraft mod let's plays
- Kill count world records in mobile games

These may occasionally have an oblique angle (e.g., "rise of Indian mobile esports" story) but should never be covered directly.

- Owner is a "cinema lover" — movie/game tie-ins perform very well (RE Movie easter egg)
- Owner is a coder — AI/tech/automation angles resonate (Hacknet, "ye game tumhe replace karega")

- Hinglish is natural, not forced — mix as you speak, don't translate
