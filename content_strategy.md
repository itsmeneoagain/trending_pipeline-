# Channel Content Strategy & Profile

This document details the complete content strategy, positioning, tiers, and angles for our Hinglish discovery gaming channel. Our automated AI curator (`src/curator.py`) reads this specification to find, score, and draft creative video hooks.

---

## 🎙 Channel Identity & Voice
* **Format**: Direct-to-camera, casual, no-filter, narrative-first voice.
* **Target Audience**: Indian gamers (YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels / 90s explainers).
* **Tone**: Mystery-building, cinematic hooks, avoiding roasts or standard tutorials.

---

## 🎯 Positioning: The "Discovery" Advantage
We focus on **underrated, indie, and overlooked games and industry stories** that mainstream Indian creators ignore. We do **not** compete head-on with mainstream reviews or gameplay videos.

---

## 📊 Content Tiers

### 💎 Tier 1 — Hidden Gems
* **Focus**: High-quality underrated/under-covered games with virtually zero Hindi/Hinglish coverage.
* **Examples**:
  * *Pragmata*
  * *Clair Obscur: Expedition 33*
  * *Mina the Hollower*
  * *Cairn*
  * *Mixtape*

### 🔍 Tier 2 — Big-Search Adjacency
* **Focus**: Oblique, adjacent, or sideways takes on massive trending titles (e.g., *GTA 6*, *Pokémon*).
* **Strategy**: We never cover the obvious news; we find the hidden angle, industry drama, or cultural easter egg.

### 📜 Tier 3 — Themed Lists
* **Focus**: Custom-curated "best of" or "most X" lists surrounding a specific gaming genre, mood, or mechanic.

---

## 🎬 Core Content Angles

| Angle | Strategy & Execution |
|-------|----------------------|
| **Game Introductions** | *"You've never heard of this game — here is why it will blow your mind."* |
| **Topical Explainers** | Deep dives into systemic questions (e.g., *"Why big studios ignore Indian dubs"*). |
| **Breaking News** | Time-sensitive leaks or announcements (always framings like *"reportedly"* or *"leaked"*). |
| **Pop-Culture Tie-ins** | Cinematic easter eggs, movie connections (e.g., *Resident Evil*, *Mortal Kombat*). |
| **Profile Pieces** | Stories of the designers, developers, or executives behind the titles. |
| **Gaming History** | Reliving classic turning points or forgotten retro milestones. |

---

## ⚖️ AI Selection & Scoring Rubric

The discovery pipeline ranks trends based on five key metrics:
1. **Discovery Value**: Is it genuinely overlooked in the Hinglish space? (Higher = Better)
2. **Hook Potential**: Is there a story, mystery, or cinematic twist we can build a hook around?
3. **Indian Audience Pull**: Search interest, pricing, specs, or local relatability.
4. **Angle Fit**: Can it cleanly execute one of our 6 core content angles?
5. **Timeliness**: Freshness of news / leaks.
